The origin of
YZLUXE HOTEL

Yzluxe Hotel is situated in Sanya, Hainan. Its name comes from the initials Y, Z, L of the three founders’ surnames, with Luxe representing its core luxury. United by a shared vision of refined elegance, the founders were inspired by Sanya’s unique blend of coastal serenity and tropical glamor. Yzluxe Hotel embodies the elegance of Y, the devotion of Z, and the luxury of L. Crafted with understated opulence and meticulous details, it offers an exclusive coastal retreat of distinguished comfort and style.
